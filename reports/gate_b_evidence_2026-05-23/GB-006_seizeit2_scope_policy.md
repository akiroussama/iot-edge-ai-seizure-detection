# GB-006 SeizeIT2 Scope Policy

Reviewer: O. Akir
Review date: 2026-05-23
Decision: APPROVED_EXCLUSION

## Scope

This evidence closes the SeizeIT2 track-completeness action for Gate B by
approving a conservative exclusion policy for unsupported SeizeIT2 claims.

## Decision

SeizeIT2 is retained only for the locally verifiable/subcohort readiness track
at this stage. Full-cohort or missing split/task/modality claims are excluded
from main benchmark claims until official manifests and expected counts are
available.

## Rationale

The local guardrail rerun does not provide enough official full-cohort evidence
to support SeizeIT2 full-cohort benchmark language. Excluding unsupported tracks
is more scientifically defensible than silently treating missing modality,
split, task, or expected-count evidence as complete.

## Consequence

SeizeIT2 may appear in the paper as a readiness-limited or subcohort artifact
unless and until official split manifests, expected counts, and modality/task
coverage are available and validated. Full-cohort claims remain excluded from
main citable tables.
