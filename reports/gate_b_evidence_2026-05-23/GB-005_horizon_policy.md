# GB-005 MSG Horizon Policy

Reviewer: O. Akir
Review date: 2026-05-23
Decision: RESOLVED

## Scope

This evidence closes the MSG horizon-feasibility action for Gate B. It limits
which horizons may support headline claims.

## Decision

Main MSG claims are restricted to horizons that satisfy the pre-freeze
feasibility criteria. Weak horizons are retained only as feasibility-negative or
appendix analyses and are not used for headline benchmark claims.

## Rationale

The local guardrail rerun identified multiple horizons with insufficient valid
window or event-coverable support. Keeping these horizons in the main table
would turn feasibility limitations into apparent model failures or inflated
coverage claims. The paper should instead distinguish:

- main citable horizons;
- feasibility-negative horizons;
- source-review-required horizons;
- appendix-only diagnostic rows.

## Consequence

Final Gate C benchmark rows must carry horizon eligibility metadata. Any horizon
that fails feasibility or source-review criteria remains outside headline
claims, even if exploratory predictions exist.
