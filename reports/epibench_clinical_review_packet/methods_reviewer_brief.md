# Methods Reviewer Brief

You are asked to review whether EpiBench Dataset Evidence Cards and claim ceilings are reproducible from
the evidence provided.

## Your Task

For each dataset package, score the MTS and DSI items independently, record the evidence path supporting
each score, assign a dataset tier, and assign the maximum claim ceiling before model metrics are inspected.

## What To Focus On

- raw-to-processed traceability;
- split manifest determinism;
- leakage audit evidence;
- missingness and artifact accounting;
- license and provenance;
- whether rubrics are sufficiently unambiguous for independent scoring;
- whether claim gates are mechanically derived rather than author-selected.

## Required Output

Complete `dataset_review_form.csv`, list ambiguous rubric items, and propose wording changes that would
make a future reviewer reach the same score without author consultation.
