# EpiTwin-Open v0.1 — 48h milestone

## Contribution

Convert public wearable seizure datasets from retrospective detection toward leakage-safe calibrated forecasting.

## Implemented

- SPH/SOP forecasting labels
- ictal and postictal exclusion flags
- event-level sensitivity
- FAR/hour and FAR/day
- time-in-warning
- lead time
- Brier score and ECE
- random rate-matched predictor
- patient-wise and temporal split utilities
- leakage audit utilities
- synthetic smoke-test pipeline
